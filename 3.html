BCom

BCom (Bachelor of Commerce) Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) is a three-year undergraduate program that focuses on commerce, finance, accounting, and business management. The course provides students with knowledge of financial systems, taxation, banking, and business operations. Students develop analytical, accounting, and business decision-making skills that are essential for careers in commerce and finance.

Key Subjects

  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Economics
  • Taxation
  • Business Law

Career Opportunities

  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Banking Professional
  • Tax Consultant

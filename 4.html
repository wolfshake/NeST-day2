MBA

MBA (Master of Business Administration) Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate program that focuses on advanced business management and leadership skills. The course helps students understand strategic planning, marketing, finance, and organizational management. MBA prepares students for leadership roles in companies and improves decision-making, managerial, and entrepreneurial skills.

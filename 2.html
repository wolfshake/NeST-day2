BTech

BTech (Bachelor of Technology) Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a four-year undergraduate engineering program that focuses on technology, innovation, and problem-solving skills. The course provides students with strong knowledge in engineering principles, programming, electronics, and modern technological developments. Students learn how to design, develop, and maintain technological systems used in industries. The program also improves analytical thinking, technical skills, and project development abilities. BTech prepares students for careers in engineering fields or for higher studies like MTech or MBA.

Key Subjects

  • Programming and Data Structures
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Computer Networks
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • System Engineer
  • Network Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • Technical Consultant

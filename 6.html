BA Psychology

BA Psychology is a three-year undergraduate program that focuses on the study of human behavior, emotions, and mental processes. The course helps students understand how people think, feel, and behave in different situations. Students develop skills in counseling, research, and psychological analysis.

Key Subjects

  • Introduction to Psychology
  • Developmental Psychology
  • Social Psychology
  • Counseling Psychology

Career Opportunities

  • Counselor
  • Psychology Researcher
  • HR Specialist
  • Clinical Psychologist (with higher studies)
